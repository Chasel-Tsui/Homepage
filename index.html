Chang Xu
PhD Student · ECEO Lab, EPFL, Switzerland

I am a PhD student (2024–) at the ECEO Lab, EPFL, supervised by Prof. Devis Tuia (EPFL) and Prof. Sebastian Schemm (University of Cambridge). During the PhD, my research focuses on reconstructing high-resolution Earth system dynamics with physical consistency, to benefit applications such as a reliable forecasting of extreme weather events.

Prior to EPFL, I received both my B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees from the Electronic Information School, Wuhan University, under the supervision of Prof. Wen Yang, where I worked on object detection and localization with a focus on tiny object detection.
